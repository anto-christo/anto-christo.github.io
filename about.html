Hi, my name is Anto Christopher. I'm a final year Bachelor of Computer Engineering student from Don Bosco Institute of Technology, Mumbai University. I have immense passion for coding and that is what I spend most of my time on. For the past few years, I've been developing web, mobile applications and libraries/frameworks as my side projects. I host most of these projects on GitHub to do my bit for the Open Source community. I believe Open Source is an excellent way for students like me to learn and develop industry grade applications. Also, Open Source provides a platform to help provide free software to a large group of people. I'm just a beginner when it comes to Open Source development, but I aim to build Open Source softwares and make an impact.
